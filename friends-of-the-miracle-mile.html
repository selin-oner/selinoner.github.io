(team of two)

Project Overview

Friends of the Miracle Mile is a California 501(c)3 nonprofit organization devoted to connecting their resources to community causes. The nonprofit supports a variety of nonprofits and programs to serve many communities in the Pasadena area. This particular project was for their upcoming expungement program.

My team consisted of members of Code the Change, a USC student orgnization. I worked alongside another designer, a team of developers, a tech lead, and a project manager. We created a website that would help inform people about the expungement program they offer and provide resources for job readiness for the future.

Tools Used

Figma
Adobe Illustrator
Adobe Photoshop

Key Takeaways

1. Be flexible.
When working with a client, it's important to be open to their feedback and deliver the product according to their needs.
2. Collaborate and communicate.
Because I was working with a large team, I needed to help create an evironment that supported open communication.
3. Be compassionate.
This project required me to understand a specific target audience and dive deeper into their narrative.
