hey! nice to meet you :)

I'm an undergraduate student at USC studying Cognitive Science and minoring in Business and User Experience. By finding the intersection of design, technology, and innovation, I aim to create dynamic interactive experiences that generate answers to specific challenges.

I draw my inspiration from diverse components of everyday life, or anything that gives me the opportunity to share who I am.

My design journey has evolved as I continuously learn versatile skills, adapt to different environments, and am introduced to new fields that peak my interest. I'm currently involved in UI/UX design, but am enthusiastic about getting into Project Management as well!
