Vibe Check

role

product designer

timeline

feb 2022

objective

create a product in 48 hours

Project Overview

Vibe Check is an online platform that analyzes users’ Instagram posts, visualizes their overall vibe, and gives suggestions on how to increase engagement with their profile. Creators see which emotions and colors followers see the most on their feed and get tips on how to grow their account. You simply enter your Instagram username to get a Vibe Check!

We worked in a team of four--the others taking on front-end and back-end development. We first prioritized brainstorming and deciding on which features to include. Then, I created the low-fidelity model. However, after my team realized the time constraint would limit the development of some features, we simplified Vibe Check to be a more concise and compressed version of our original idea.

Overall, my team and I are proud of the project we created in the short amount of time we were given and how we used new technologies to make a tool that creators can use to develop follower interaction strategies. Vibe Check could make a difference in creators’ lives where the platform makes it easier to come up with ways to increase engagement using data analytics.

Key Takeaways

1. Adapt.
Working in a fast paced environment really showed me how frequently things can change and how that I should change my work accordingly.
2. Manage your time.
When working on a project that has a specific time limit, I needed to manage my time well.
3. Communicate.
Even though I was working in a small team, this still required communication for seamless collaboration.
